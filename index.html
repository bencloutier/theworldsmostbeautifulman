2K12CR

About

2K12CR cranks out killer music with comedic lyrics, poking fun at bronies, hipsters, pop culture, fringe culture, and everything in between. The band’s affinity for music spans multiple genres and fuels albums that have something for everyone: Metal, R&B, Country, Indie, Rock, K Pop, Emo, Rap, and the list goes on.

Bio

Initially a solo project of Ben Cloutier’s, 2K12CR transformed into the musically mind-melting, lyrically absurd, dynamic duo of today, when David Menendez joined in 2015, after collaborating on one of Ben’s solo albums. Channeling their shared sense of humor and instrumental prowess, 2K12CR (2012 Cloutier Records) released their first album in 2016, titled Nothing 2 Lose. This was followed by the 2017 album release of Very Good Bad Boys. Playing at lounges, bars, coffee shops, and house parties, they have developed a small, yet mighty, ever-growing Austin fan base who can be heard screaming along that they don’t wanna clean their rooms either. Whether you’re into women’s armpit hair, delusional redditors, manspreading, or scrapbooking, the 2K Boys have you covered.
